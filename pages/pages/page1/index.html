ONITSUKA TIGER

Onitsuka Tiger is a Japanese sports fashion brand started in 1949 by Onitsuka Shōkai (鬼塚商会, Onitsuka Co., Ltd), a sports shoes company founded by Kihachiro Onitsuka. Onitsuka Shōkai changed its name to Onitsuka Co., Ltd.

Mexico 66 Yellow/Black

Design

Beloved by fashionable folks of varying ages and aesthetics, Mexico 66s are true style chameleons. The Yellow/Black colorway is particularly striking and recognizable, often associated with Bruce Lee.

Material

Typically crafted from a combination of leather and suede, offering both durability and a premium feel. The iconic black tiger stripes on the sides are a distinctive design element.

Features

Ultimate 81

History

The ULTIMATE 81 is part of Onitsuka Tiger's range inspired by the 1980s running shoes. Originally launched in 1981, this shoe is all about stability and lightness.

Material

Made from a combination of synthetic leather, mesh, and suede, providing a balance of breathability, flexibility, and durability.

Color Options

Available in various color combinations, with some popular options including the classic white/blue/red and more modern interpretations.

Features

The iconic Onitsuka Tiger stripes are prominently displayed on the sides, contributing to the sneaker's distinctive look. Features a unique heel-cross reinforcement for added stability and support.
